A bit about me...

I am from a small town in Central Massachusetts. My journey into design and tech is a bit unorthodox compared to most. I was first exposed to design and tech in high school, but it wasn’t until after I graduated from college that I decided it was what I wanted to do for my career. I started taking online courses in order to further develop my skills and learn new ones.

That decision has opened up a whole new world for me and a fresh way for me to express myself and my story. I love the challenge that designing and coding brings to the table. They force me to think critically, attack problems head on, focus on the details, and force me to be intentional in all that I do. There is no better feeling than solving a problem in your own code or completing a project, it’s an adrenaline rush I can’t stop chasing.
