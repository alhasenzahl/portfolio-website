Abril Fontface Project

This was one of the very first projects that I ever coded. During my Front End Developer Career Blueprint through SkillCrush.com, this was one of our very first projects that we were given. The instructors gave us the PSD design file and we were tasked with turning the design into code. This project was also my first hint at the challenges and frustrations that can come with coding sometimes. However, it was also my first taste of the overwhelming sense of pride that comes with fixing your own code and finally getting it right. There is no better feeling.
