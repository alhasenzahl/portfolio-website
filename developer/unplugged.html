Unplugged Music Website Project

This website presented many challenges and forced me to step away and come back to it several times. Even though the project brought many challenges and frustrations, I was extremely happy when I was able to complete the project. There is a great sense of accomplishment and pride I feel when I not only complete a project, but when I am able to show some persistence and fix my own code.
