Welcome to the Mad, Mad World

This project was an event poster and CD artwork that I created for a local musician's concert. The event posters were hung up in the area to promote the concert. He also put a CD together of all of the songs from the show to be sold at the concert. I designed the cover and back tray artwork for the CD. He wanted a wild, wild, west theme for all of the artwork and had a clear vision for what he was looking for, my job was solely to make his vision come to life exactly how he pictured it.
