My Portfolio Website

When I was designing my portfolio, I went through several ideas, versions, and revisions before landing upon the design that you are currently viewing. However, I am extremely proud of the final design that I created. Each part was intentionally put together in order to be a direct reflection of not only me as a designer, but me as a person. I ultimately chose a simple layout that has an informal, laid back look and feel. I enjoy bright colors and placing them on neutral tones allows them to shine brighter without overwhelming the design. I chose green because it represents personal growth and change, which is what this project represents to me. It represents me stepping outside of my comfort zone and taking a step towards becoming the person that I want to be. When it came to creating a logo to represent myself, I wanted something simple and clean. But, I also wanted something that was imperfect, asymmetrical, and had some quirks that gave it character. These are all characteristics I would use to describe myself and I therefore intentionally placed them within my entire design.
