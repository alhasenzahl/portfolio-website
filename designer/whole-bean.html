Whole Bean Coffee Shop Branding Project

The Whole Bean was a pretend new business that was given to me during my SkillCrush Visual Designer Career Blueprint class. The project included creating an entire visual brand for the shop top to bottom and from scratch. I decided to create a sincere and wholesome brand identity centered around the idea of making connections and community over a cup of coffee to create lifelong memories. The typescale, color scheme, and logo are all elements that I intentionally created so each would complete a specific piece of the brand. The FaceBook header and landing page are culminations that bring each element together to showcase the brand identity in its entirety.
