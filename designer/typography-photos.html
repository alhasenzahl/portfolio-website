Making images from words...

When I first started my design and tech journey, I began following other designers on Instagram in an attempt to connect with others in the field as well as gain inspiration and ideas for my own work. One of them was a foreign designer, whose work immediately caught my eye and inspired these two designs of mine. The first design is one I created for a local musician and one of his current projects he is working on with his brother. I used the name of their project to create the image of a guitar and made the cable spell the world the where it plugs into the guitar. I was a summer camp counselor for three years at an all girls sleep away camp in Maine. The second design was to commemorate my campers last summer as campers and their time at camp overall. The bunks the girls sleep in are named after birds, with the oldest girls living in "Flamingo" bunk. I created the body of the flamingo out of the word flamingo and used the year '17 to create the legs.
