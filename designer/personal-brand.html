Personal Branding Project

One of the very first projects assigned during my Visual Designer Career Blueprint from SkillCrush.com was to create an entire brand personality for ourselves. I had to create a typescale, color scheme, and logo as well as design a portfolio website that I could use to display my work for potential clients or employers. I would describe myself as simple and informal, yet passionate and driven, so when it came to creating the elements for this project I wanted them to reflect these characteristics. I chose a typescale that had fun and informal display texts with a complementary serif paragraph text. I am a fan of using bold, bright shades of color that really stand out, however, I also like to use them against neutral colors to make sure they aren't to overpowering. For this reason, when picking colors for this project I went with a bright shade of a relaxing teal, as well as black and white. For the website layout, I decided to use two popular UI trends of big typography and large photos to create the basis of my designs.
